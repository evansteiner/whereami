Finding your location...

What is this?

A lightweight app that uses your device’s location and public APIs to instantly tell you what city you're in.

Why build this?

Navigation apps are great at directions, but not always helpful when you're simply curious about where you are. While driving through interesting neighborhoods or landmarks, I often wondered, “What city is this?”—and found no easy answer. This app is a simple “push button, get city name” tool built to satisfy that curiosity.

What APIs does it use?

  • Your device’s GPS for latitude and longitude
  • OpenStreetMap.org for converting coordinates to a readable address
  • Google Maps for displaying maps

What makes it special?

It’s intentionally minimal—just HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. All API calls are free and unauthenticated. We don’t track or store anything. It’s real-time info, on demand.
